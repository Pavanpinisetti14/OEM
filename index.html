Vulnerabilities Overview

OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturer) websites are essential platforms where manufacturers provide comprehensive information about their products and services. These sites typically feature detailed product specifications, technical support resources, software updates, and documentation necessary for both consumers and businesses. They also offer access to marketing materials, pricing information, and a support channel for troubleshooting. OEM websites play a crucial role in ensuring users have all the resources needed to effectively utilize and maintain their products, while also serving as a hub for partners and resellers to access relevant information.
